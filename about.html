About

Background

My experience is based in many different types of digital media: web development, web design, photography, graphic design, animation, video, motion graphics, and 3D modeling.

My first professional role was at a photo studio in New York, where I assisted with photo shoots and did post-production photo editing. The next year I taught English in Malaysia, and then designed websites at an advertising agency in Massachusetts when I got back home. Now I'm on the digital experience team at John Hancock Investments in Boston, working on an extensive website redesign project and towards an overall digital transformation.

I'm always looking to be inspired and challenged; it's important to me to never stop learning, growing, and moving forward.
